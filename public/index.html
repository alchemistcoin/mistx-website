On 05/26/2021, the Alchemist project announced the launch of mistX - a decentralized exchange based on Flashbots technologies.

One of the most important problems of the Ethereum network, and DeFi in particular, is the damage caused by the actions of front-running bots and the associated crisis of MEV, which amounts to hundreds of millions of dollars worth of loss to users.

The main losses are incurred by ordinary, non-technical users of DEXs such as Uniswap, Sushiswap and other AMM Dex exchanges in the form of front-run or ‘sandwiched’ transactions.

mistX is the first time in Ethereum history that FlashBots is available to regular users for front-running protection and "no success, no fee" transactions.
